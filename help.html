    Limit trading is a pending order transaction, you can set your own purchase price, and other prices fluctuate to your set price when you can deal

    Market transaction is not set buy/Sell price, you can guarantee a timely deal, just enter the total amount you want to buy or want to sell the total amount can be. Market transactions can guarantee immediate transaction, but the transaction price is not guaranteed to coincide with the prevailing market prices.
